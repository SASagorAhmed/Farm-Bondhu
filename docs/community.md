# FarmBondhu Community Guide

FarmBondhu Community is the platform's knowledge-sharing, discussion, and support space. It helps farmers, buyers, sellers, pet and livestock owners, vets, doctors, learners, and admins exchange practical questions, answers, experiences, and updates.

Community is not a replacement for VetBondhu, MediBondhu, emergency care, admin support, or official marketplace dispute handling. It is a shared learning and discussion area where users can ask, answer, react, save, and report content responsibly.

---

## Purpose

Community exists to make practical knowledge easier to find and share inside FarmBondhu.

It supports:

- Questions and answers from real users
- Practical farm, animal, pet, marketplace, and business discussions
- Urgent help discovery for problems that need quick attention
- Category-based browsing
- Animal-based filtering
- Comments, answers, reactions, saves, and sharing
- Moderation and reporting for unsafe or inappropriate content

The goal is to help users learn from each other while still directing serious medical, veterinary, payment, legal, or account issues to the correct professional service.

---

## Why Community Exists

Many users face similar problems:

- A farmer sees an unknown symptom in cattle or poultry.
- A pet owner is unsure if a sign is urgent.
- A seller wants advice about product listing, pricing, or buyer questions.
- A buyer needs guidance before purchasing farm or pet products.
- A new user wants to learn from experienced users.
- A vet, doctor, or expert wants to share general education.

Community gives these users one place to discuss, search, and learn before or alongside using dedicated services such as VetBondhu, MediBondhu, GreenBondhu Marketplace, or admin support.

## Main Features

### Feed

The feed shows active community posts and supports discovery tabs:

- **Latest:** newest active posts
- **Questions:** question and help-request posts
- **Urgent:** urgent or expert-needed posts
- **Unanswered:** questions/help requests with no answers yet

### Filters and search

Users can narrow the feed by category, animal type, and search text.

### Post detail

Each post detail page can include:

- Original post content
- Author information
- Comments
- Answers
- Best-answer status
- Reactions
- Save/bookmark state
- Shared post preview
- Link preview where available
- Edit/delete actions for the owner or moderator
- Report option for inappropriate content

### Reactions and saves

Users can react to helpful content and save posts for later reading.

### Comments and answers

Comments support discussion. Answers are best for question/help-request posts where users need a clear response.

### Reporting and moderation

Users can report unsafe, spammy, misleading, abusive, or inappropriate content. Admins/moderators can review and moderate community activity.

## Priority Guidance

Use priority carefully:

- **Normal:** general questions, learning, discussion, tips, and non-urgent issues
- **Urgent:** time-sensitive problems where quick community attention is useful
- **Expert needed:** questions where a vet, doctor, admin, or experienced user should respond

Do not mark every post urgent. If an animal or person is in danger, contact the correct emergency service immediately instead of waiting for community replies.

## How To Ask A Good Question

A good Community question is clear, specific, and safe.

Include:

- A short title that explains the issue
- Animal/product/farm context when relevant
- When the problem started
- What changed recently
- What you already tried
- Photos or videos if useful and safe
- Location context only if needed, without sharing full private address
- Whether you already contacted a vet, doctor, seller, or admin

Example:

```text
Title: Cow not eating and has swollen left side since morning

Details:
- Cow, about 4 years old
- Not eating since morning
- Left side of abdomen looks swollen
- Breathing faster than normal
- No medicine given yet
- Need advice on what to do before local vet arrives
```

This is better than writing only "cow sick help" because it gives the community and vet users enough context.

---

## How To Answer Responsibly

When answering:

- Be respectful and practical.
- Say if you are sharing personal experience, not professional advice.
- Ask for missing information instead of guessing.
- Do not prescribe medicine, injections, or dosage unless you are qualified and the platform allows that role.
- Recommend VetBondhu for animal-health issues that need a vet.
- Recommend MediBondhu for human-health issues that need a doctor.
- Recommend admin/support for account, order, payment, access, or moderation problems.
- Recommend immediate local emergency help for severe symptoms.

Unsafe answers can harm animals, people, buyers, sellers, or the platform. Keep advice clear and responsible.

---

## Urgent And Emergency Guidance

Community can help users notice problems, but it is not an emergency service.

### Animal emergencies

Do not wait for community replies if an animal has:

- Severe breathing difficulty
- Heavy bleeding
- Poisoning
- Seizures
- Sudden collapse
- Severe injury or fracture
- Difficult birth
- Bloat or rapidly swollen abdomen
- Inability to stand

Use VetBondhu for first guidance if available, but contact the nearest vet or animal hospital immediately.

### Human medical concerns

For human illness, injury, medicine, or emergency symptoms, use MediBondhu or local medical services. Community comments should not replace professional medical consultation.

### Marketplace and payment problems

For order disputes, payment, delivery, returns, fraud concerns, or account access problems, use the official marketplace/admin support paths rather than relying only on community replies.

---

## Privacy And Safety Rules

Do not post:

- Passwords or OTP codes
- Full home address
- Private phone numbers if not necessary
- Payment card, mobile banking, or transaction secrets
- Other users' private information
- Prescriptions belonging to unrelated users
- Abusive, hateful, threatening, or harassing content
- False medical, veterinary, or marketplace claims
- Spam or misleading promotions

Photos and videos should be useful, respectful, and safe. Avoid posting graphic content unless it is necessary for understanding an animal-health issue, and describe it clearly.

---

## Reporting And Moderation

Report content when it is:

- Harmful medical or veterinary advice
- Spam or scam activity
- Harassment or abuse
- False marketplace claims
- Privacy violation
- Inappropriate image or language
- Duplicate or misleading content

Moderators/admins may review, hide, edit status, or remove content according to platform rules. Post owners may edit or delete their own content where the UI allows it.
