# FarmBondhu Customers Guide

FarmBondhu serves many customer types across one green SaaS enterprise ecosystem. It is not only an agriculture app and not only a marketplace. A customer may use one service only, such as MediBondhu doctor consultation, VetBondhu animal care, GreenBondhu Marketplace buying, seller shop tools, farm management, community learning, or admin support.

This guide explains the major FarmBondhu customer groups, what they need, which module serves them, and how their privacy and access should be handled.

---

## What Is A FarmBondhu Customer?

A FarmBondhu customer is any person, provider, seller, professional, or operator who uses one or more FarmBondhu services.

Examples:

- A farmer managing animals and farm records
- A buyer ordering products from GreenBondhu Marketplace
- A pet owner requesting VetBondhu guidance
- A patient booking a MediBondhu doctor
- A seller running a shop
- A vet giving animal-care consultation
- A doctor giving human-care consultation
- A learner reading Community posts
- An admin managing approvals and support

FarmBondhu should present itself as a multi-service platform where users can enter through the service they need.

## Service-By-Service Customer Journeys

### Farm management customer

Farm management customers are usually farmers, farm owners, or farm staff. They need organized records for daily farm operations.

Typical journey:

1. Sign up or log in.
2. Access the farm workspace.
3. Add farm and animal records.
4. Track feed, health, production, mortality, sales, and finances.
5. Use marketplace, VetBondhu, Community, or AI help when needed.

Primary value:

- Better records
- Faster animal and farm tracking
- More organized decisions
- Connection to marketplace and veterinary support

### GreenBondhu Marketplace buyer

Marketplace buyers may be farmers, pet owners, general customers, or businesses. They do not need to use farm management to buy products.

Typical journey:

1. Browse GreenBondhu Marketplace.
2. Search products or open category lanes.
3. Compare product details, price, wholesale rules, and seller information.
4. Add products to cart.
5. Checkout and track orders.
6. Chat with seller if product questions are needed.

Primary value:

- Product discovery
- Cart and checkout
- Order tracking
- Seller chat
- Access to farm, pet, animal-care, human-care, and machinery-related products

### Seller and vendor customer

Seller customers use FarmBondhu as a business platform. They may sell farm products, pet supplies, animal-health items, machinery, or related products.

Typical journey:

1. Request or receive seller capability.
2. Set up shop profile.
3. Add products and inventory.
4. Configure pricing and wholesale rules.
5. Receive orders and buyer messages.
6. Fulfill orders and manage reviews.
7. Use seller photo editor for product and shop visuals.

Primary value:

- Shop storefront
- Product management
- Buyer chat
- Orders and fulfillment
- Reviews and shop trust
- Payout and earnings workflows where enabled

### VetBondhu animal-care customer

VetBondhu customers include farmers, pet owners, livestock owners, and anyone who needs veterinary guidance for animals.

Typical journey:

1. Choose VetBondhu.
2. Select or book a vet consultation.
3. Prepare animal details, symptoms, photos, videos, and medicine history.
4. Join waiting room and video consultation.
5. Follow vet advice and prescription.
6. Review consultation history and follow up if needed.

Primary value:

- Faster first veterinary guidance
- Video-supported animal review
- Prescriptions and care records
- Support for livestock, pets, poultry, and farm animals

Important: VetBondhu does not replace in-person emergency treatment. Severe animal emergencies need nearest veterinary help immediately.

### MediBondhu healthcare customer

MediBondhu customers are human patients and doctors. A person can use MediBondhu even if they never use farm management or marketplace.

Typical patient journey:

1. Open MediBondhu.
2. Find available doctors.
3. Choose online or chamber consultation if available.
4. Book appointment.
5. Join waiting room and consultation room.
6. Review prescription and chat history after completion.

Typical doctor journey:

1. Maintain doctor profile and availability.
2. Set schedule windows.
3. Review appointment requests.
4. Start online consultation.
5. Consult patient and manage prescriptions.

Primary value:

- Available doctor discovery
- Appointment booking
- Online consultation
- Doctor schedule management
- Human-care prescription and history

Important: MediBondhu is for human healthcare. It must stay separate from VetBondhu veterinary workflows.

### Community and learning customer

Community customers may be any FarmBondhu user who wants to ask, answer, learn, or share experience.

Typical journey:

1. Open Community.
2. Browse latest, questions, urgent, or unanswered posts.
3. Filter by category or animal type.
4. Ask a question or share an experience.
5. Comment, answer, react, save, or report content.

Primary value:

- Practical knowledge sharing
- Peer support
- Learning from farmer, seller, owner, vet, doctor, and admin experience
- Discovery of useful questions and answers

Important: Community advice should not replace professional veterinary, medical, legal, payment, or admin support.

### Admin and support customer

Admins and support users are internal or trusted platform operators. They protect service quality and help users.

Typical journey:

1. Review access requests and approvals.
2. Moderate users, sellers, products, and reports.
3. Support customers through platform support channels.
4. Review marketplace, email, reports, seller lanes, and admin operations.
5. Keep user privacy and service quality stable.

Primary value:

- Platform governance
- Moderation
- Trust and safety
- Customer support
- Operational visibility

## Access And Capability Model

FarmBondhu uses role and capability-based access. A customer does not need every module.

Simple examples:

- A buyer may only need `can_buy`.
- A seller needs seller/shop capabilities.
- A farmer needs farm-management capabilities.
- A vet needs VetBondhu consultation capability.
- A MediBondhu doctor needs doctor-specific approval and schedule access.
- An admin needs platform management capabilities.

This model helps keep each customer in the correct workspace and prevents private data from appearing in the wrong account.

---

## Privacy Expectations

Each customer type should only see their own relevant data.

| Data type | Privacy expectation |
| --- | --- |
| Orders | Only buyer, seller, and authorized admins should see relevant order data |
| Notifications | Notifications should be account-specific and not leak across users |
| Chat | Conversation access should be limited to participants and authorized support/admin roles |
| Consultations | VetBondhu and MediBondhu consultation data should stay scoped to participants |
| Prescriptions | Prescriptions should only be visible to authorized users in the relevant service |
| Farm records | Farm and animal records should stay under the correct owner/account |
| Admin reports | Moderation and reports should be restricted to admin/moderation access |

Privacy is a core enterprise requirement. A customer using one service should not accidentally see another customer's orders, notifications, chats, or consultation records.

## When To Use Which Module

| Need | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Buy products | GreenBondhu Marketplace |
| Sell products | Seller/vendor tools |
| Manage farm records | Farm management |
| Ask general farm or animal questions | Community |
| Need veterinary consultation | VetBondhu |
| Need human doctor consultation | MediBondhu |
| Create product/shop images | Seller photo editor |
| Report unsafe content | Community report/admin moderation |
| Need order/account/platform support | Support/admin tools |
| Need urgent animal care | Nearest vet/clinic plus VetBondhu for first guidance if available |
| Need urgent human medical care | Local emergency care/doctor; MediBondhu when suitable |

## Example Customer Journeys

### Buyer-only customer

A user joins FarmBondhu only to buy pet supplies. They browse GreenBondhu Marketplace, add products to cart, place an order, track delivery, and chat with the seller if needed. They may never use farm management.

### Patient-only customer

A user needs a doctor consultation. They open MediBondhu, find an available doctor, book an appointment, join the online room, receive advice/prescription, and review the chat history later.

### Pet owner customer

A pet owner notices their dog is not eating. They ask a Community question for general guidance, then use VetBondhu to consult a vet if symptoms continue or become serious.

### Farmer customer

A farmer manages cows and goats in the farm workspace, buys feed from GreenBondhu Marketplace, asks Community questions, and uses VetBondhu when an animal needs professional guidance.

### Seller customer

A seller opens a shop, lists products, edits product photos, responds to buyer chat, manages orders, reviews earnings, and requests support when needed.

### Admin customer

An admin reviews access requests, marketplace moderation, reports, seller lanes, support messages, and platform operations to keep FarmBondhu safe and trusted.

---

## Customer Success Principles

- Let customers enter through the service they need.
- Do not force every user into agriculture workflows.
- Keep FarmBondhu as the umbrella platform.
- Keep GreenBondhu Marketplace as the commerce hub.
- Keep VetBondhu and MediBondhu separate.
- Protect privacy between accounts and roles.
- Use clear module names so customers know where to go.
- Guide emergency cases to real emergency care, not only online discussion.
- Keep documentation simple enough for users and accurate enough for developers.
